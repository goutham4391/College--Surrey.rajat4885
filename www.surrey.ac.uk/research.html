Research at the University of Surrey

The University of Surrey is a world-class, research-led university committed to research excellence. Our research seeks to answer global challenges, drive innovation and deliver real-world impact.

Within this section, you will find information on our research themes along with our newly launched Research and Innovation Strategy. We’re always looking to expand our research partnerships.
