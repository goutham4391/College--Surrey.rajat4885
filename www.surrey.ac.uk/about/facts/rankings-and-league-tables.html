Rankings and league tables

We’re committed to the continued improvement of our excellent research, teaching, employability and student experience. Rankings provide independent recognition of areas we’re proud of as a university, and the progress we’re constantly striving to deliver.

Employability rankings

First for employability

We are University of the Year for Graduate Employment in the The Times/Sunday Times Good University Guide 2022.

Highly skilled graduates

Top 10 in the UK for highly skilled employment for UK domiciled, undergraduate, full time graduating students in the Graduate Outcomes Survey 2022.

Best careers service

Best University Careers Service at the National University Employability Awards 2022.
